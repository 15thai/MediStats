Making More Easier

As health services have improved, information about them have become harder to find and manage. Such is the case with procedure count; while data on the number of procedures hospitals conduct is publicly available, it is very diffifuclt to find and aggregate. This is a shame, as such information can be used to help people make better judgements on the hospitals they choose to go to.

Here, we hope to be able to present the data in a simpler way by focusing on the number of procedures completed by the providers. The computations are based off of data from the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services website. All you have to do is type in a procedure and your ZIP code you'll be all set.

Morgan Mendis, Vadim Kudlay, and Anh Thai
